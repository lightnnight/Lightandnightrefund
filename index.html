CBT Top-Up Refund Rules
Cumulative tier-based calculation · 30 Membership Points = 1 Polaris
  • 1
    Up to 20,000 Membership Points (inclusive) — Refund at 200% based on the conversion rate of 30 Membership Points = 1 Polaris.
  • 2
    From 20,001 to 50,000 Membership Points (inclusive) — Refund at 150% based on the conversion rate of 30 Membership Points = 1 Polaris.
  • 3
    From 50,001 Membership Points and above — Refund at 100% based on the conversion rate of 30 Membership Points = 1 Polaris.
*Any decimal results in the calculation will be rounded up to the next whole number.
**Membership Points will not be refunded — they are used solely for conversion calculation and tier determination.
Illustrative Example
First 20,000 MP · Tier 1 · 200%
20,000 ÷ 30 = 666.67 → rounded up = 667 Polaris
667 × 200% = 1,334 Polaris refunded
→ 1,334 Polaris
Next 5,000 MP (20,001–25,000) · Tier 2 · 150%
5,000 ÷ 30 = 166.67 → rounded up = 167 Polaris
167 × 150% = 250.5 → rounded up = 251 Polaris refunded
→ 251 Polaris
